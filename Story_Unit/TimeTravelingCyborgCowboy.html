The Choice
“Thanks for saving me back there, partner” The man introduces himself as Clant Westwood,
Clint Eastwood’s long lost, time traveling, cyborg cowboy, half twin brother. And he offers
you the choice of going on adventures with him, returning you home, or staying in this time.


Do you take him up on his offer and jon him on his adventures? Have him return you back to your normal time? Or do you walk away?
