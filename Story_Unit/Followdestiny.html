Following Destiny
You decide to follow the voice, but as you walk towards it it seems to get fainter and fainter,
until you turn a dark corner to see a lone shop cart with an old man as its keeper. You ask
the man why he was calling your name, and he said he could sense that you were here,
and that the prophecy told many years ago might be fulfilled by an individual such as yourself.


Do you listen to the story of the prophecy? Or do you walk away?
