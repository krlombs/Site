Ghost Cube

The cost cube is a modification of a regular 3x3x3 rbuks cube that allows it to transfrom into different shapes, allowing only one solution. The mechanism in the cube is a skewb mechanism, which allows it to turn while not in the regular cube shape.

These are the sizes it comes in:
